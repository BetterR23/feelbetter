What I Do

I am a Web Developer proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React, experienced in building responsive websites and Web Apps. I'm also comfortable with some wireframes like Bootstrap and Tailwind. I'm passionate about creating high-quality, well-designed websites, and I'm always eager to learn new skills and technologies.

I am very experienced in Designing logos, brand guidelines, and other branding materials that help businesses establish their identity as well as designs for print, including brochures, flyers, and business cards. My design philosophy is simple: "To create designs that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional." I believe that good design is about more than just making something look pretty; it's about solving problems and meeting the needs of clients.
